# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

The EVA community is committed to a respectful, technically rigorous, and inclusive environment. Participants must:

- discuss ideas and evidence rather than attacking people;
- accept constructive review and correct mistakes transparently;
- respect privacy, authorship, licensing, and security boundaries;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sexualized conduct;
- avoid publishing credentials, private documents, or vulnerability details.

## Scope

This policy applies to repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, community events, and public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove harmful content, reject contributions, restrict participation, or permanently ban repeated or severe violations. Conduct concerns should be reported privately to the maintainers through an available private repository contact channel. Security vulnerabilities must follow `SECURITY.md`.

Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and focused on community safety.
